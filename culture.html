🎉 Culture of Assam 🎉

Traditions, Festivals, Food and Heritage

Bihu Festivals

Rongali Bihu marks the Assamese New Year and celebrates spring. Bhogali Bihu is associated with harvest and feasting. Kongali Bihu is observed with simplicity and prayers for a good harvest. Together these festivals represent the agricultural heart of Assam.

Assam Silk

Muga Silk

Golden silk unique to Assam and famous worldwide.

Eri Silk

Known for warmth, softness and durability.

Pat Silk

Elegant silk traditionally used in Assamese attire.

Traditional Food

Khar

A signature Assamese dish prepared with alkaline ingredients.

Masor Tenga

A light and tangy fish curry loved throughout Assam.

Pitha

Traditional rice cakes prepared during festivals.

Jolpan

A popular traditional breakfast combination.
