Long Biography of Assam

1. Assam is located in northeastern India and serves as the gateway to the region.

2. The capital of Assam is Dispur while Guwahati is the largest city.

3. The Brahmaputra River is the lifeline of the state.

4. Ancient Assam was known as Kamarupa.

5. The Ahom Kingdom shaped much of Assam's history.

6. Assam tea is famous throughout the world.

7. Kaziranga National Park protects the one-horned rhinoceros.

8. Manas National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

9. Bihu is the most celebrated festival of Assam.

10. Muga Silk is unique and internationally recognized.
